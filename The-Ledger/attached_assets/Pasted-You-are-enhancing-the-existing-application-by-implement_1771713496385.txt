2️⃣ DERIVED CALCULATIONS (Display Only)

Use front-end computed values only.

Profit:
profit = contract_value - total_cost

Margin %:

Budget Burn %:
burn = (total_cost / contract_value) * 100

Remaining Budget:
remaining = contract_value - total_cost

Invoice Collection %:
collection_rate = (paid_amount / invoiced_amount) * 100

DO NOT persist these values to backend.
They are UI-calculated only.
