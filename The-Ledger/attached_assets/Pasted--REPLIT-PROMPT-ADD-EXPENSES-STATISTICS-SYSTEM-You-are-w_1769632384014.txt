2. EXPENSES — CORE CONCEPT

Expenses are operational costs, not accounting records.

They may come from:

Workers (travel, materials, accommodation, etc.)

Equipment (rentals, repairs, servicing)

Job-specific purchases

Expenses may:

Be tied to a Job

Or be general business expenses

3. EXPENSE ROLES & PERMISSIONS
Workers

Can submit expense requests

Cannot approve expenses

Cannot edit approved expenses

Project Managers

Can:

Create expenses directly

Approve or reject expense requests

Assign expenses to jobs

Admin / CEO

Full visibility

Can view statistics and totals

Can override if necessary

Use existing RBAC system — do not invent a new one.

4. EXPENSE CREATION & APPROVAL FLOW
Expense Request (Workers)

Workers can:

Submit an Expense Request with:

Title / description

Amount

Category (travel, materials, equipment rental, etc.)

Optional job link

Optional receipt upload

Status defaults to Pending Approval

Expense Approval (Project Managers)

Project Managers can:

View pending requests

Approve or reject them

Add notes on approval/rejection

Once approved:

Expense becomes immutable to workers

Expense counts toward statistics

Direct Expense Creation (Managers)

Project Managers & Admins can:

Create expenses directly (auto-approved)

Use this for:

Equipment rentals

Repairs

Business costs

On the Expenses page (or a simple Statistics section within it), add:
