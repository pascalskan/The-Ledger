### CORE IDEA
The Ledger is a trade-business operations management tool built for small-to-medium businesses that organise work into **jobs/projects**, assign **workers** and **equipment**, track schedules, prevent conflicts, and maintain clear audit logs of all operational changes. It is built on a flexible, editable database core that lets businesses customise fields, input real-world operational data, and generate actionable views of what is going on. It is NOT a CRM, not full ERP, and not a marketplace. It is a **controlled, operational tool that prioritises clarity, simplicity, and control over complexity.** The product is delivered as a **hosted SaaS under the Omnisoftware brand**.

### DETAILED FEATURES (per guidance document)
1. Schema-driven database with editable fields and expandable entities  
2. Core operational entities:
   - Workers (with role tags and custom roles)
   - Jobs / Projects with unique readable IDs  
   - Equipment / Assets with status/availability  
   - Clients linked to jobs  
3. Role-based access control (RBAC) with tenant isolation (each company is a separate tenant)
4. Conflict detection for overlapping worker/equipment assignments
5. Saved operational views (daily/weekly schedules, idle equipment, unassigned jobs)
6. Audit logging for all CRUD and assignments
7. Map page that shows job markers with colour-coding per status
8. Invoice creation tied to Jobs and Clients, with templates, editable fields, and send/print support
9. Complete UI flows with working Create/Edit/Delete, role screens, and functional navigation
10. Example Business tenant seeded with demo data while real accounts start bare-bones
