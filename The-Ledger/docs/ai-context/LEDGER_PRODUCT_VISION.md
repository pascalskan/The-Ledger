WHAT IS THE LEDGER
The Ledger is an Operational Intelligence Platform built specifically for field service, trade, maintenance,facilities management, labour, security, cleaning, and construction businesses.
The Ledger exists to bridge the gap between operational reality and financial reality.
Most businesses operate with disconnected systems:
Workers perform work
Project managers coordinate work
Finance teams reconstruct events later
Accounting systems become overloaded with operational responsibility
The result is poor visibility, delayed decision-making, inaccurate profitability reporting, and inconsistentfinancial records.
The Ledger solves this by transforming operational activity into structured financial intelligence.
The Ledger is not accounting software.
The Ledger is the operational layer that sits between field operations and accounting systems.

THE LEDGER PIPELINE
Operational Event → Worker Submission → Review Centre → Approval → Financial Normalization →Business Intelligence → Accounting Synchronization
Nothing may bypass this pipeline.
WHY THE REVIEW CENTRE EXISTS
The Review Centre is the heart of The Ledger.
Most systems allow operational activity to directly affect financial records.
The Ledger intentionally prevents this.
Nothing becomes financially real until approved.
This preserves:
Accuracy
Accountability
Auditability
Profitability visibility
Approval transforms operational activity into financial reality.
